RAG and reports quick reference for agents.

Purpose
- Prepare later expansion for 13F filings, domestic brokerage briefings, reports, summaries, and retrieval-style research pages.

Current Priority
- Future phase after core dashboard, stock detail, portfolio, and first real data screens work.

Constraints
- Preserve source provenance for every parsed report, summary, and retrieved answer.
- Store raw document metadata separately from parsed text and generated summaries.
- Do not treat generated summaries as verified financial advice.
- Prefer small, inspectable ingestion steps before automated report collection.
- Keep Korean brokerage reports and US filings as separate source families until normalization rules are clear.

Pitfalls
- RAG quality cannot be assumed from embedding alone.
- PDF parsing and table extraction will require source-specific checks.
- Copyright and redistribution constraints may limit what can be stored or shown.
- 13F data is delayed and should not be presented as current trading activity.
